Los Angeles security guard rates are not a single number. In most markets, **unarmed** coverage is the baseline option for retail, offices, and controlled sites, while **armed** coverage costs more because it requires additional qualifications, training, and liability management.

## What typically moves price in LA

- **Coverage type**: static post vs patrol vs hybrid models.
- **Hours**: overnight and weekend shifts can affect availability and pricing.
- **Site risk**: high-value inventory, open perimeters, and prior incidents change staffing recommendations.
- **Supervision and reporting**: quality programs include field leadership and usable daily documentation.

## What you should ask for in a quote

A professional Los Angeles security company should provide clear assumptions: number of posts, hours, armed vs unarmed recommendations, overtime rules, and any equipment or vehicle fees (if patrol is included). If the quote is vague, you are comparing incomparable options.

## The honest takeaway

If your goal is sustainable deterrence—not just a uniform at a desk—optimize for **accountability** and **clear post orders**. A slightly higher hourly rate with better supervision often costs less than theft, downtime, and repeated vendor churn.
