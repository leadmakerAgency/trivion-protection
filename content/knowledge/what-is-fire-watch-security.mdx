**Fire watch** is a temporary security and safety function used when fire protection systems are impaired, or when hot work and other operations require continuous monitoring beyond normal staffing.

## Common situations

- Fire alarm or sprinkler system impairments during maintenance or construction
- Hot work schedules where a dedicated watch is part of the safety plan
- Occupied buildings where impairment windows must be actively monitored

## What professional fire watch looks like

Effective fire watch is not “standing around.” It typically includes:

- Continuous rounds and clear logging
- Prompt escalation when conditions change
- Coordination with on-site maintenance and leadership

## Los Angeles operations note

Many LA sites combine **construction activity**, **tenant operations**, and **tight windows** for impairments. The right plan aligns staffing to the actual impairment schedule, so you are not paying for coverage you do not need, and you are not under-protected during the highest-risk periods.
