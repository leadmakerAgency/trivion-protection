## **How to Vet Your Event Security Provider**

Selecting a security partner is a critical decision that impacts the safety and success of your event. Before finalizing a contract, use this vetting process to ensure your chosen firm meets the high standards required in Los Angeles:

- **Verify Licensing:** Ensure the company holds a valid **Private Patrol Operator (PPO)** license from the California Bureau of Security and Investigative Services (BSIS).
- **Request a Security Playbook:** Ask how they plan for your specific site. A top-tier firm will offer a customized plan, including emergency evacuation routes, medical response protocols, and crowd-flow management.
- **Check Background Standards:** Inquire about their recruitment. Look for firms that hire professionals with prior law enforcement, military, or elite private sector experience.
- **Assess Scalability:** Ensure the firm has the "bench depth" to scale up staffing if your event attendance exceeds initial projections.
- **Transparency and Insurance:** Always request a Certificate of Insurance (COI) and demand a contract that clearly outlines the scope of work without hidden fees.
