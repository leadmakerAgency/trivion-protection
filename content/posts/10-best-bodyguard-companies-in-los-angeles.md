Selecting an executive protection vendor in Los Angeles requires a deep understanding of California regulatory frameworks and specialized liability exposure. Elite firms insulate their clients from civil and legal liability by employing professionals from specialized backgrounds, including retired municipal law enforcement officers, former federal agents, and elite military veterans.
